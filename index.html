A post-partisan business helping other businesses, entrepreneurs, and community groups leverage technological change to connect with their audiences.

More than ever, having an online digital identity and presence is one of the biggest determining factors to economic opportunity and mobility.

The success of entrepreneurs and small businesses depends on being able to meet customers where they are, and providing them with the value they are seeking.

For better or for worse, those customers are increasingly spending their time and money online – meaning, you also need to be online if you hope to beat out or join the competition.

Unfortunately, for many small businesses in already marginalized communities, this is still far out of reach.

Necessity breeds innovation — but this is only possible in a system that provides sufficient access to the tools, resources, and stewardship to implement ideas into reality.
